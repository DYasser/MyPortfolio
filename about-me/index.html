About me. I'm a developer, software passionate and linguist who has been progressing and improving his skills since 2018. I enjoy developing and work every day on becoming better.
Scroll
Casablanca is far from Silicon Valley, but growing up there gave me a unique perspective on life. As a Ukrainian-Moroccan, I was fortunate to experience two distinct cultures and start my language journey early, learning multiple languages from a young age. I earned my Bachelor's in Software Engineering from Wuhan University in Wuhan, China, where I developed a deep passion for various aspects of the field. My hard work led to an internship at CERN in Geneva, Switzerland, and ultimately a fully-funded scholarship to pursue a Master's in Computer Science at the University of Padua in Padua, Italy. Currently, I am living in Hamilton, Canada working as Software Testing Engineer with Evertz and improving my development skills on my own time by introducing myself to new technologies and developing personal projects.
Serial language-botherer Besides coding, I have a deep interest in linguistics and psychology of language acquisition. I was lucky to be born in Morocco where we get out of highschool with at least 3 languages and even luckier to be Ukrainian that gave me the opportunity to have an additional language from my youngest age. My decision to pursue a bachelor's degree in China was driven by my fascination with the language. I was challenged, but I loved how learning Chinese reshaped my thinking. It was my fourth language, and while COVID interrupted my time in China and prevented me from mastering it, I still made significant progress and continue to learn. After learning Chinese, Italian felt much easier to master, and I picked it up in no time. I'm now focused on becoming fluent in Italian, then plan to fully dedicate myself to mastering Chinese. After that, I'll tackle Spanish as my next language challenge, further expanding my linguistic skills.
Traveling Outside of my work I like to spend as much time as I can away from my laptop, travelling and getting out with friends. The countries I was in until now: Morocco, Ukraine, Turkey, China, HongKong, Philippines, Switzerland, France, Spain, Italy, Poland, Germany, Canada.
